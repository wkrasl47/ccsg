Community Competition Support Group

Empowering Community Excellence

Community Competition Support Group (CCSG) is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit that empowers community excellence through fundraising campaigns. We serve as a fiscal conduit for qualifying amateur sports, academic achievement, artistic performance, and peer-to-peer charitable initiatives.

Past and current candidates include senior Wii Bowling tournaments, youth academic leagues, and resident-led artistic showcases. Donors may direct support to eligible programs by using the Organizer Contact form or by confirming their target community’s participation.

Group members collaborate to define funding priorities, build donor relationships, evaluate grant candidates, shape media outreach, and contribute legal, financial, technical, and governance expertise.
